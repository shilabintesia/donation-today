Critical disease

You can help poor people by providing life-saving medical care. This includes sponsoring their medical supplies and sometimes surgeries to transform their lives and give them hope to live. We have been able to perform over 4,26,850 free of cost critical disease and corrective surgeries to date, with your support. Donate money to our NGO and help us in raising funds for poor families.

Education and skill development

Thousands of children, teens and adults in India are left to fend for themselves, either because they are orphans or their family does not have sufficient funds to support their education. Donate money to our NGO to help the poor and needy persons who are looking for a ray of hope to uplift their life. The right education and skill development can help them get decent jobs and become contributing members of society.

Corona Relief Fund

The COVID-19 outbreak has resulted in many children getting homeless, and many families sleeping hungry. Our NGO is providing free of cost cooked food, ration kits, and also medical supplies to such families and individuals who have lost their loved ones and are in dire need of support. We must unite to help them fight the deadly COVID-19 second wave in India by reducing the burden on them. Donate money to help the poor and needy and fight the menace of poverty.
